# Glossary

## Core Terms

### UGC

User-generated content. In this project, it refers to creator-produced content used to drive affiliate or commerce outcomes.

### Creator

An individual content producer whose performance is tracked in the dashboard.

### Creator Operations

The management discipline of reviewing creator performance, directing interventions, and coordinating follow-up actions.

### GMV

Gross Merchandise Value. The total sales value attributed to a creator or content source, according to the business's chosen source and attribution rules.

### MTD

Month-to-date. A rolling value from the start of the current month to now.

### 7D

Seven-day rolling window.

### 30D

Thirty-day rolling window.

### Tier

A business-defined classification such as bronze, silver, or gold used to segment or reward creators.

### Tier Eligibility

The state of meeting the documented criteria for a tier. This should be rule-driven, not manually implied.

### Active

A creator state indicating that the creator is currently being managed and should appear as active in operational views.

### Inactive

A creator state indicating the creator is not currently active in the program or should not be treated as current-operating inventory.

### At-Risk

A derived operational label suggesting the creator may need attention because of inactivity, declining metrics, or recent negative signals.

### Activity

A structured operational record attached to a creator, such as a win, loss, observation, or adjustment.

### Win

A positive notable event or observation about a creator's recent performance.

### Loss

A negative notable event or observation about a creator's recent performance.

### Observation

A descriptive note that may not clearly be positive or negative but is still worth preserving in the creator history.

### Adjustment

A recorded change to strategy, incentive, direction, or classification intended to influence creator performance.

### Linked Account

A social platform account associated with a creator, such as a TikTok or Instagram handle.

### Rollup

A current operational summary value calculated for a defined window, such as 7D GMV or MTD post count.

### Snapshot

A historical fact record capturing metric values at a specific point in time or for a specific date.

### Source

The origin of a metric or record, such as manual entry, webhook, API, or import.

### Freshness

The recency of a metric relative to the expected update cadence.

### Role

The authorization category assigned to a user, such as admin, manager, viewer, or creator.

### Scope

The boundary of data a user is allowed to access.

### Manager-Owned Scope

The set of creators assigned to a specific manager.

### Viewer

A read-only user who may inspect data within an approved scope but cannot mutate records.

### Creator Self-View

A future mode where a creator can view only their own data and only the subset intended for creator visibility.

### Export

A generated file, typically CSV, containing a filtered and authorized slice of creator and metric data.

### Realtime

For this project, the operational behavior where subscribed users see updated data without manual page refresh. This does not imply every metric is sourced live from the originating external system.

### System Of Record

The authoritative application or dataset the team relies on for current operational truth.
